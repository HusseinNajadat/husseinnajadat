About Me

Hussein Najadat, a Senior Account Executive at Golin MENA, brings over 4 years of expertise in public relations with a specialization in corporate and government sectors in the UAE.

Proficient in corporate communications planning, navigating the media landscape, and content development. Hussein possesses a comprehensive skill set in his role and continues to excel in delivering impactful communication strategies.

Prior to joining Golin, Hussein serviced clients such as the Sheikha Bodour Executive Office, Emirates Publishers Association, Department of Finance - Ajman, Sharjah Digital Department, Estonia Government, Sharjah Book Authority, and the Department of Government Relations, Sharjah.
